Food for a family

Food For Free improves access to healthy food through establishing innovative programming and partnerships to overcome barriers and strengthen the community food system. We envision a future where everyone in our community – regardless of age, income, or ability – has consistent access to fresh, healthy, delicious food.

Shelter for a family

Rotational shelter is Family Promise’s original shelter model. The rotational shelter model engages a network of community partners, often faith communities, to house families with children in different locations on a rotational basis. This innovative model— mobilizing volunteers to help house families in community space—has helped over one million children and adults.
